What is South film industry called?
Southern Indian Cinema is used to refer collectively to the four distinct film industries of Tollywood (Telugu), Kollywood (Tamil), Mollywood (Malayalam) and Sandalwood (Kannada) as a single entity. The list is based on the conservative box office estimates as reported by industry sources.

Tollywood makes a lot of movies each year that are renowned for their excellent production values, extravagant song and dance routines, and gripping plotlines. Some of the most gifted and adaptable actors in the Indian film industry have come from Telugu cinema over the years.

Prabhas

Prabhas Raju Uppalapati, Prabhas is a popular Indian actor who appears in Telugu cinema. He is the nephew of the veteran actor of the Telugu film industry, Uppalapati Krishnam Raju. In the year 2015, Prabhas starred in the movie, Baahubali, which is one of the highest grossing movies of Indian cinema and later reprised his titular role in Baahubali 2: The Conclusion (2017), which became the first Indian film in the history of Indian cinema to gross 1000 crores INR at the box office. The huge star that Prabhas is, the actor not only has a huge fan base across India but also over the years has earned several nick names from his fans including Mr. Perfect, Pubsi and Prabha.

Ram Charan

Konidela Ram Charan is an Indian actor, producer, and entrepreneur who primarily works in Telugu films. One of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema, he is the recipient of three Filmfare Awards and two Nandi Awards. Since 2013, he has featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list.
Charan made his acting debut with the action film Chirutha (2007), a box office hit, winning the Filmfare Award for Best Male Debut – South. He rose to prominence starring in S. S. Rajamouli's fantasy action film Magadheera (2009), the highest-grossing Telugu film of all time at the time of its release, winning the Filmfare Award for Best Actor – Telugu.
